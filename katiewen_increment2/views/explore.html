Explore

MonoMuse is a contemporary museum dedicated to the intersection of art, technology, and human creativity. We believe that art and technology together can transform how we see the world.

Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire curiosity and creativity by bringing together innovative artists and cutting-edge technology in an accessible, welcoming space for everyone.

Who We Serve

MonoMuse welcomes visitors of all ages and backgrounds — from students and families to artists, researchers, and technology enthusiasts.

Our History

Founded in 2005, MonoMuse has grown from a small gallery into a world-class institution. Here are some of our key milestones:

  • 2005 — MonoMuse founded in Pittsburgh, PA
  • 2010 — Opened our first permanent digital art wing
  • 2015 — Launched our interactive installations program
  • 2020 — Introduced virtual tours during the pandemic
  • 2024 — Expanded to a new 50,000 sq ft facility
