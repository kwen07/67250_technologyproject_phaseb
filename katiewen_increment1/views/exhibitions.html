Exhibitions

The Digital Frontier

Step into the future with our flagship exhibition exploring how artificial intelligence and machine learning are reshaping the boundaries of creative expression. Featuring works from over 20 international artists, The Digital Frontier challenges visitors to question what it means to create in the age of machines.

Echoes of Nature

Echoes of Nature is an immersive installation that blends environmental data with generative art. Sensors placed in forests and oceans around the world feed live data into visual displays, creating a constantly evolving portrait of our planet. A meditative and thought-provoking experience for all ages.

Human/Machine

Human/Machine examines the evolving relationship between people and technology through sculpture, photography, and interactive digital pieces. Visitors are invited to participate in live demonstrations and add their own mark to collaborative artworks throughout the gallery.
